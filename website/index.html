Getting Started
Up and running
in minutes
Whether you're a buyer, agent, or service pro — getting started on Loop is straightforward.
1

Create Your Account

Sign up as a buyer, renter, agent, or service provider. Each role gets a tailored onboarding flow and dashboard.

2

Set Your Location

Allow Loop to detect your city automatically, or browse any of our supported markets. Relevant listings load instantly.
